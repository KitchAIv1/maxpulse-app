## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MaxPulse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the MaxPulse mobile application (the "App").

**MaxPulse is exclusive to verified Maximum 88 customers and distributors.**

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Health Data
We collect the following health-related information:
- **Steps:** Daily step count tracked via device motion sensors
- **Hydration:** Water intake logs (manual entry)
- **Sleep:** Sleep hours (manual entry or HealthKit integration)
- **Mood Check-Ins:** Mood levels, energy levels, stress levels, and optional journal entries

### 2.2 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Age, gender, height, weight, BMI
- **Medical Information:** Medical conditions, allergies, medications (optional)
- **Mental Health Data:** Wellness assessment responses

### 2.3 Device Data
- **Motion & Fitness Data:** Pedometer data, activity levels
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data:** App interaction patterns, feature usage

### 2.4 AI Coach Conversations
- **Chat History:** Conversations with the AI Coach feature
- **Symptom Reports:** Health symptoms shared with the AI Coach
- **Health Recommendations:** Personalized wellness advice generated

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 How We Store Your Data
- **Database:** Securely stored in Supabase (SOC 2 Type II compliant)
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
- **Access Control:** Row Level Security (RLS) ensures only you can access your data
- **Location:** Data stored in secure cloud infrastructure

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Encrypted authentication credentials
- Secure token storage using industry-standard practices
- Regular security audits
- No health data logged or exposed in analytics
- Two-factor authentication support (coming soon)

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### 5.1 Supabase (Database & Authentication)
- **Purpose:** Store your health data and manage authentication
- **Data Shared:** All app data (encrypted)
- **Privacy Policy:** https://supabase.com/privacy

### 5.2 OpenAI (AI Coach)
- **Purpose:** Power the AI Coach conversational feature
- **Data Shared:** Chat messages (anonymized, no personally identifiable information)

- **Privacy Policy:** https://openai.com/privacy

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access Your Data
- View all your health data anytime in the app
- Request a copy of your data: support@maxpulse.com

### 7.2 Delete Your Data
- Delete specific entries within the app
- Request complete account deletion: support@maxpulse.com
- Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

### 7.3 Export Your Data
- Export your health data in portable format
- Available in Profile → Settings → Export Data

### 7.4 Withdraw Consent
- Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings
- Revoke motion sensor permissions in iOS Settings
- Discontinue use of AI Coach feature

### 7.5 Opt-Out of Communications
- Unsubscribe from emails (link in every email)
- Disable push notifications in app settings
